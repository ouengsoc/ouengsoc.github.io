Capture the flag

OUEngSoc is proud to be hosting the first ever varsity "Capture the Flag". Sponsored by BAE Systems, this day-long event involves completing a wide variety of team-based challenges against teams from Cambridge University Engineering Society.

All ranges of coding/programming abilities are welcome.

Challenge categories include: Crypto, Reverse Engineering, Binary Exploitation, Network, Packets, Web, Problem Solving and Trivia.

For more information and to sign up, please visit the Facebook event page or contact us at president@ouengsoc.org.
