Celonis Data-mining Workshop
MT 2019
How can we ensure that airplanes are on time, supermarket shelves are full, and patients don’t have to wait? Celonis uses big data analytics to reconstruct company processes. They are the market leader for Process Mining technology, recent tech unicorn and one of the fastest growing companies in Europe. Oxford Alumna Angela Gebert and Pauline Zollver will be giving a hands-on workshop about how big data can be used to understand the inner workings of market giants like Vodafone, BMW or Uber and to create extraordinary process experiences for everyone.

IMC Trading Workshop
MT 2019
Find out what it takes to be a market maker! During our Trading workshop we organize with @IMC Trading, you’ll develop a trading strategy and test it on real-world data. This is a perfect opportunity to better understand the complexities in designing trading algorithms, and what it’s like to do this at IMC. Meet IMC’s traders and developers, ask all your questions about their business, life at IMC and more.

Frictionless Finance Workshop with Celonis
HT 2020
We are pleased to announce that OUEngSoc and the Oxford Guild are hosting a workshop event for the tech 'unicorn' Celonis, a Process Mining Company, following our workshop with them last term.

The financial sector is full of high-value transactions and interactions with customers and partners on a daily basis. However, organizations often struggle to keep track of their inner workings and invoices get paid twice, customers get impatient or transactions get lost.

Discover together with tech-unicorn Celonis how financial management from working capital management to audit and customer service can be innovated with process analytics.

As one of the fastest-growing tech companies in Europe Celonis grew from a student start-up into the global market leader and pioneer of digital transformation as well as recent tech-unicorn realising millions of savings for global players like Uber, Citi, Siemens or Vodafone.

Come along to find out more about this exciting field!

Food and drink provided.
